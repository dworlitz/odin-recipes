Odin Recipes



  • Lasagna alla Bolognese

    First we will start with the special italian bolognese lasagna. Gosh vikings loved that even before it existed. Take a closer look and you will see what I am talking about!


  • Grilled Fish Steak

    An everyday meal for the true viking from the true north beyond the wall. Fish were part of they daily protein replenishment so they can chop trees down with their bare hands. One fish, one tree, one fish one tree and so on!


  • Flank Steak Barbecue

    Meat meals were always an special ceremony, huge part of the war rituals and they got lots of it! The more meat they ate the higher the chance of win battles! If you dont eat meat than you cannot go to war and put everything to shame!
